How do I make new profiles or open old ones?

To make a new profile, click on the "New Profile" tab and fill out the form
similar to when you started.

To retrieve an old profile, click the "Open Profile" tab and find the desired .dat file.

Why do the "Milestones" and "Modules" tabs not work?

These tabs become active once you have a HUB file imported.
To do so, choose a valid hub file (in .xml format).

Why is there only one .xml file I can choose?

Currently we are working on fully implementing custom profiles. Soon to Come!
